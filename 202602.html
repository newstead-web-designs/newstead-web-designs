Web Accessibility

Published: 09 February 2026

Web accessibility ensures websites can be used by everyone, including people with disabilities. An accessible website is easier to navigate, clearer to understand, and more inclusive for all users. Following the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) helps create websites that are usable, flexible, and future-proof.

Accessibility is not just a technical requirement. It improves usability, supports search engine optimisation, and creates a more professional experience for every visitor, regardless of device or ability.

Tip: Build accessibility into your website from the start. It is easier to implement early and benefits all users.

What WCAG Covers

  • Perceivable: Content can be seen or heard. Add text alternatives for images, captions for videos, and clear fonts and colours.
  • Operable: Make sure visitors can navigate your site with a keyboard or assistive tools without confusion.
  • Understandable: Ensure instructions, forms, and content are easy to follow and consistent.
  • Robust: Websites should work across devices, browsers, and with screen readers.

Following these principles makes your website welcoming to everyone.

Why Accessibility Matters

  • Reach more users, including those with visual, hearing, or motor impairments.
  • Improves usability and makes content easier to find via search engines.
  • Reduces legal risks and helps comply with accessibility regulations.
  • Enhances overall user experience, making your site friendly and professional.

A small accessibility improvement can make a big difference in how visitors interact with your content.

How We Make Websites Accessible

  • Use clean HTML, CSS, and scripts for a simple, readable structure.
  • Organise headings, lists, and menus clearly so users can navigate easily.
  • Provide alt text, captions, transcripts, and colour contrast checks.
  • Test with keyboard navigation, screen readers, and other assistive tools.

By following these steps, your site works well for all visitors.

Benefits of Accessibility

  • Better engagement and longer visits from all users.
  • Boosts SEO and search visibility.
  • Builds trust and professionalism for your brand.

Accessible websites are easier for everyone to use, reducing frustration and improving satisfaction.
